Fried Rice

Description

This is a vegan fried rice recipe that is easy and cheap to make.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • one block of tofu
  • one white onion
  • one pack of white mushrooms
  • one clove of garlic
  • soyaki sauce

Steps

  1. Cook white rice and let cool for 30 minutes
  2. Press tofu while rice is cooking/cooling
  3. Heat large skillet with oil
  4. Dice onion and mushrooms and add to pan
  5. Crush garlic and add to pan
  6. Crumble tofu into pan
  7. Add rice and stir all contents of pan while it cooks for five minutes
