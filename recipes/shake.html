Bulking Shake

Description

This is a smoothie that I drink when I am trying to gain weight and need extra calories/protein after the gym.

Ingredients

  • half a cup of quick cook oats
  • two cups of soy milk
  • one spoonful of peanut butter
  • two spoonfuls of cream of coconut
  • one scoop of v-whey protein powder
  • one scoop of pea protein
  • half of a banana
  • half of an avacado
  • a handful of frozen berries of your choice

Steps

  1. Add oats to blender and blend for 30 seconds
  2. Add remaining ingredients and blend for 30 more seconds
