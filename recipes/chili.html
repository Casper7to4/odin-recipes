Vegan Chili

Description

This is a bowl of my famous vegan chili. I first learned the recipe years ago from a popular vegan chef on Youtube. I made it for my now wife on one of our very first dates.

Ingredients

  • one red onion
  • one yellow onion
  • one clove of garlic
  • three bell peppers (red/yellow/orange)
  • six jalapeno peppers
  • three cans of diced tomatoes
  • one can of black beans
  • one can of kidney beans
  • one can of chili beans
  • spices (cinnamon,cumin,cayenne pepper,chili powder, and salt)
  • vegan meat crumbles (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat large pot with oil
  2. Dice both onions and add to pot
  3. Crush garlic and add to pot
  4. Let cook for 2 minutes
  5. Cut bell peppers and add to pot
  6. Cut jalapeno peppers and add to pot
  7. Stir and let cook for several minutes
  8. Add canned beans and tomatoes to pot
  9. Add all spices to pot and stir
  10. Add vegan meat crumbles to pot (optional)
  11. Place lid on pot, reduce heat to simmer and stir occasionaly while it cooks for at least 20 minutes
